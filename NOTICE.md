## Templot5 source basis

Certain railway calculations, dimensional references, and validation data were
informed by Templot5 revision 556b, copyright Martin Wynne and OpenTemplot
contributors, distributed under GNU GPL version 3 or later.

The exact reviewed source snapshot is available from the
[Templot5 SourceForge files page](https://sourceforge.net/projects/opentemplot/files/)
as `t5_files_556b_06_feb_2025.zip`. Its identity and the relationship to this
project are recorded in [`reference/PROVENANCE.md`](reference/PROVENANCE.md).

This notice records the source basis and preserves upstream credit. It does not
